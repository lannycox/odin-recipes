Hoisin-Teriyaki Stir Fry

A blend of sweet and savory sauces complements lean strips of beef and fresh veggies.

Ingredients
  • 1 package beef strips
  • 1 small yellow onion
  • 1 large stalk celery
  • 1 large carrot, julienned
  • 1 small red bell pepper, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, peeled and chopped
  • 1/2 cup chopped white mushrooms
  • 3/4 cup hoisin sauce
  • 1/2 cup teriyaki sauce
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • steamed short-grain white rice (prepare to your liking)
Steps
  1. Preheat a large frying pan on medium heat, add 4tbsp of your preferred cooking oil.
  2. Add meat, season with salt and black pepper, and cook until meat is slightly browned.
  3. Add onions and sautee until translucent, taking care not to burn.
  4. Add peppers, celery, carrots, garlic, and mushrooms, cook while stirring about 2 minutes.
  5. Add sauces and cook until veggies have softened (usually about 5-10 minutes).
  6. Serve over steamed white rice. Sesame seeds can be used as an optional garnish.
