One-Pot Chili and Cheese

The absolute peak of meal-prep - downright addictive.

Ingredients
  • 1 1/2 cups rice
  • 1 package ground beef
  • 1 large tin diced tomatoes
  • 1 tin dark red kidney beans
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp chili powder
  • 3 tbsp chipotle hot sauce
  • medium cheddar cheese, shredded or chunked
Steps
  1. Drain kidney beans and tomatoes, reserving 1 1/2 cups liquid, adding water if necessary.
  2. Brown meat in a wok or large frying pan, adding salt and chili powder.
  3. Add liquid and 1 1/2 cups of rice, bring to boil for 10 minutes.
  4. Stir in kidney beans, tomatoes, and chipotle sauce, reduce heat to simmer.
  5. Simmer until chili is reduced and rice is plump. Serve in bowl and top with cheese.
